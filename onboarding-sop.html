Phase 2
Intake & Access
Day 1–2 — before kick-off call
Client completes intake form
17 questions: business basics, existing accounts, goals, competitor info. If not received in 24h, follow up by text.
CLIENT
Review intake answers before kick-off
Highlight anything unclear. Flag missing access. Pull up their GBP and competitor before the call.
YOU
Research their top competitor
Check GBP reviews, map pack position, website, any visible ads. Bring specifics to kick-off.

Phase 3
Kick-off Call

0:00

0:05

0:10

0:20
Set the 90-day goal — "By [date] we want [X]. Measured by: [metric]." Write it down, send it in your follow-up.
0:25
Comms rhythm — weekly email update, or text for quick questions? Confirm now.
0:28
Q&A — surface any concerns now, not in a 10pm text in Week 3.

Phase 4
Delivery & Rhythm
Week 1 onward

3–5 sentences: what was done, what's next, any decisions needed from them.
YOU

Reference

The 17 Intake Questions

Send via Typeform, Google Form, or Notion form immediately after contract signed.

01
Legal business name
02
Primary contact name + cell (for texts)
03
Website URL (if existing)
04
Google Business Profile URL (if existing)
05
What cities/areas do you service?
06
What are your top 3 services by revenue?
07
Do you have a Google account for business? (if yes, share email)
08
Do you have a Facebook Business page?
09
Do you have Google Analytics or Search Console set up?
10
Who is your website host? (if existing site)
11
Do you have a domain registrar login? (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
12
What does a good lead look like? (job type, job size)
13
What's your average job value?
14
How many new jobs/month would make this worth it?
15
How are you currently getting leads? (word of mouth / Google / referrals / other)
16
Who is your top local competitor? (name + website if known)
17
Anything we should know to hit the ground running?
